Study Corner

Finding a space where you can study or work at home is vitally important these days. It is undeniable that smart working and remote learning have set new rules for the internal distribution of space at home and, consequently, for furnishing.

This study corner proposal comes from the idea of offering a suitable setup for this purpose, with the goal of minimizing bulk while maintaining a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere.

H4: Wall-mounted wooden bookcase
A5: Custom wooden bookcase
S12: Wooden chair

Single Loft Bedroom
(ideal for children)

The arrival of a child at home changes everything, including the way we perceive space.

Adults often think only in square meters, while children who climb, jump, and move without limits invite us to think in cubic meters and treat height as an ally that helps expand and improve storage capacity in a kids' room, with the added value of fun.

We are experts and enthusiasts of loft beds as a furnishing solution.
