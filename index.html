WHAT WE DO

Totem is Italian design applied to ecological furniture; we design and create custom-made natural furniture systems for home and work environments.

Design
Ecological furniture designed to give value to nature, wood, with a simple, functional look, creating welcoming atmospheres, harmonious and refined.
Innovation
Since 1996 we have been studying building solutions (the joints) and refining advanced technologies (laser cutting) to obtain modern, elegant and robust ecological furniture.
Respect for nature
We use wood from forests with “controlled cutting” and whose trees are replaced, avoiding polluting materials, favoring the concept of "sustainable forestry".
For homes and offices
Totem's ecological furniture (structurally light weight, solid and durable) is suitable for setting up any type of domestic or work space space in an elegant and functional way.
Customization
Modular and interlocking natural furnishing systems to create environments according to your taste, your creativity and your needs.
Quality/price
We offer high-quality natural furniture solutions at "consumer" prices because we simplify processes and minimize advertising expenses.
